# Track Freeze

![Bitwig Freeze](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/649293987848519691/653906403647553538/Yrw7Ufc.png)

Group what you want to freeze in a group, then group that in another group. Now take the root group head and bounce it in place, then take the child group head and deactivate it to save CPU.
